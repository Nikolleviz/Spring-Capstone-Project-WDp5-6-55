The beautiful aspects of art

By Nikolle Vizcaya

Topiary Sculpture

The practice or art of training, cutting, and trimming trees or shrubs into odd or ornamental shapes.

Robotic Sculpture

Robotic installation art, a type of installation art that is programmed to respond to viewer interactions, by means of computers, sensors and actuators.

Bronze Sculpture

A three-dimensional piece of art made by pouring molten bronze into a mould, before leaving it to solidify. Bronze is an alloy of copper and tin, made by heating the two metals together and allowing them to cool.

Relief Sculpture

A wall-mounted sculpture in which the three-dimensional elements are raised from a flat base.

Kinetic Sculpture

Art from any medium that contains movement perceivable by the viewer or that depends on motion for its effect.

Welded Sculpture

An art form in which sculpture is made using welding techniques. Welding is an art, it is the quality, production, expression, or realm, according to aesthetic principles, of what is beautiful, appealing, or of more than ordinary significance.

My opinion

Sculpture is a beautiful thing. It expresses feelings that words can't describe, that people or even yourself might not even understand. Art in general does that but sculpture itself helps those feelings come to live. You can grab and touch your feelings even if it's not a living thing.
