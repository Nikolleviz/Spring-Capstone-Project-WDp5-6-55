Fresas con Crema

Fresas con Crema in english means strawberries with creams. Strawberries and creams tastes so good and for people who love deserts but want to stay healthy, and also don't want for it to take too much time making Fresas con Crema is a great way to satisfy all those needs.


Ingredients

  • Strawberries
  • Sweetened Condensed Milk
  • Sour Cream

Extra Ingredients

These ingredients are personal choice

  • Whipped Cream
  • Heavy Milk
  • Evaporated Milk
  • Vanilla

Method

  1. First wash the strawberries with just water if haven't already and cut them into small slices.
  2. After the strawberries are washed and sliced pour them into a bowl.
  3. Grab a seprate bowl and inside pour some Sweetened Condensed Milk, Sour Cream, and for personal choice: Add the Heavy Milk, Evaporated Milk, and Vanilla into the bowl as well. With a normal spoon or electrical mixer stir everything.
  4. When done add the creams into the bowl the sliced strawberries are in and mix all of it.
  5. For personal choice you can add the Whipped cream for a finish touch and at this point you have made yourself Fresas con Crema.
