Lesson 6: Conditionals Investigate

In lesson six we Investigated and modified sample apps that use conditionals and learn common programming patterns with conditionals.

What does it mean to put the most specific case first? Why is it important to put the most specific case first? What types of errors does it help avoid?
It's important to put the most specific case first because the computer reads or evaluates from top to bottom and if the most intense or specific part of the code is anywhere but the top then it could create bugs.
